Lasagna

How to make delicious lasagna

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below

ingredients

  • Meat: Ground beef or Substitue.
  • Onion:White onion.
  • Garlic:Two cloves.
  • Tomato product:Crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar:Two tablespoons of white sugar.
  • Spices:Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles:Store bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheese:Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese.

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
