Mac n Cheese

How to make delicious Mac n Cheese

Mac and cheese is a rich and creamy dish consisting of macaroni pasta mixed with a cheesy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni:1 package elbow macaroni.
  • Butter:3/4 cup butter.
  • Sour Cream:1 cup sour cream.
  • Milk:1 cup milk.
  • Cheeses:8 oz sharp cheddar cheese, 8 oz mild cheddar cheese.
  • Spices:1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper.
  • Egg:1 egg yolk.
  • Flour:2 tablespoons all-purpose flour.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Cook macaroni in boiling pot of water.
  3. Drain and transfer macaroni into large bowl stirring salt and butter with pasta.
  4. Mix butter, sour cream, sharp cheddar and egg yolk into a bowl.
  5. Stir flour, salt, cayenne pepper and milk into same mixture.
  6. Prepare baking dish with cooked macaroni and sour cream mixture.
  7. Top with mild cheddar cheese and Bake for 15 minutes.
